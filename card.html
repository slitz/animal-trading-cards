German Shepherd

In the 1920's, a German Shepherd served as the very first seeing eye dog.

  • Scientific Name: Canis lupus familiaris
  • Higher Classification: Dog
  • Life Span: 9 - 13 years
  • Temperment: Intelligent, Obedient, and Courageous

German Shepherds are among the most popular dog breeds in the world. In the USA it is the second most popular breed in terms of registrations surpassed only by the Golden Retriever. German Shepherds grow to a height of 22 - 26 inches and can weight between 50 and 88 pounds.
